# Midterm: Presenting your concepts

# Overview

Prepare and present your app concept for Winterlude, focusing on your design process and the usability of your app.

# Format

  • Delivery Method: In-class presentation during Week 07.
  • Duration: 5 minutes per student (3 minutes for presentation, 2 minutes for Q&A).

# Evaluation Criteria

  1. Presentation Skills (5 points)
  • Clarity, volume, and engagement with the audience.
  • Preparation and rehearsal evident.
  • Effective time management.
  • Competent justification of design choices and responses to questions.
  1. Content Quality (5 points)
  • Comprehensive display of concept elements:
  • Main screen, navigation, and 1-2 sub-screens.
  • Demonstration of navigation flow (e.g., home > events list > event details).
  • Clarity and realism in wireframes.
  • Inclusion of actual or realistic content in presentations.
  1. Concept Viability (5 points)
  • Adherence to UI design principles discussed in class.
  • Appropriate use of navigation models.
  • Compliance with platform design guidelines.

# Preparation Steps

  • Refine your wireframes: Using your sketch wireframes prepared in Part 2, recreate your 3 wireframes digitally using Figma. Your wireframes should be at a mid-fidelity level.
  • Tools: Figma.
  • Plan your presentation: Decide on the format (slide deck, interactive prototype).
  • Craft a 3-minute presentation: Highlight key aspects of your design, focusing on a realistic usage scenario and essential app flow. You may also include your IA to help show the structure of your application.
  • Rehearse: Practice your presentation to ensure clarity, timing, and preparedness for Q&A.

# Deliverables

  • In-Class Presentation: Conducted in Week 07.
  • Presentation PDF: To be uploaded for submission.
